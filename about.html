About Japan is Beautiful

Japan is Beautiful is a campaign created in late 2020 to promote holidays to Japan!

We at Japan is Beautiful have always had an interest for everything Japan, from its cartoons to its beaautiful scenery.

We are not a booking company, for booking your trip check the links in the footer!


We chose a Torii Gate as the icon for Japan is Beautful because as soon as you look it, you think "Japan".

Nearly every image used is from "unsplash.com".

If you have any further questions please go to the page "Contact" and send us an email!
